Your World, Your Projects

Since 1998, Hooks & Craft has provided high-quality, affordable hooks and yarn to meet every crochet-related need. Our cozy shop is located in the heart of Aylmer, just a short drive from Ottawa. Our mission is to provide clients of all skill levels with a safe space to learn the art of crochet and polish their skills. Furthermore, we believe the finest materials will always lead to outstanding projects. Join us, and let's grow the crochet community together!

Dive Into the World of Crochet

Our highly skilled team is dedicated to offering the best yarn recommendations in all of Gatineau. At Hooks & Crafts, our mission is to provide everything you need for your crochet projects, all in one place. We pride ourselves on flexible hours, competitive pricing, and outstanding customer satisfaction. Just read the plethora of wonderful reviews from our happy customers! To provide any feedback or ask questions about our products, feel free to fill out our questions and product feedback form. Don’t forget to check out our links to fun and easy-to-follow crochet tutorials to help you get started or master new techniques.

Save the Environment One Tool at a Time

At Hooks & Craft, we take our responsibility to protect the environment seriously. We’ve made it our mission to sell nothing but plastic-free yarn and tools. Yes, you heard that right! Our hooks are fully recyclable, and our yarn is crafted from the finest natural fibers. Join us in making a difference. Every small step helps save our planet!
